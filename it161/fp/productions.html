Productions

Lorca: Flamenco Poetry


debut: 2009 Alden Theater, McLean, VA

Lorca: Flamenco Poetry celebrates the passionate poetry of Federico Garcia Lorca interpreted through the music and artistry of flamenco. Lorca: Flamenco Poetry was Furia Flamenca's first production and has been one of its most successful productions garnering two Dance Metro DC awards in 2009 and an additional five finalist nominations in 2012.
