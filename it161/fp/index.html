About Furia Flamenca

Furia Flamenca, led by artistic director Estela Velez de Paredez, is an award-winning dance company that brings the ferocity and passion of flamenco to the stage and transports audiences to Southern Spain in technically refined, emotionally explosive, and dynamically choreographed dancing. Their unique style combines the traditional gypsy with the more modern flamenco to create the perfect balance. They are proud to be the most sought-after flamenco dance company in the DC-Metro area having performed in some of the most prestigious venues and festivals throughout the DC-Metro area.
