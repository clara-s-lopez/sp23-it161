Audiences Issues, Concerns, Needs Approach
Students: mostly women in their 20s-30s searching for dance classes in the DC area, accessing the web on a variety of devices. They likely have some disposable income and a car.
  • Find and register for classes and schedules
  • Locate the dance studio to attend classes
  • Learn more about instructors, Furia Flamenca in general
  • Buy shoes, outfits, and access other flamenco resources
  • Course schedule and online registration
  • Instructor biographies and general group info
  • Links to flamenco resources
  • Pictures and video of classes
  • Contact info/form
  • Map and directions to studio

  • Find and buy tickets to upcoming performances
  • Learn more about Furia Flamenca
  • Contact Furia Flamenca
  • About and bio sections
  • List of upcoming performances with links to buy tickets
  • List of previous performances
  • Reviews of performances
  • Pictures and videos of performances
  • Contact info/form
Collaborators: can be dance groups/studios, musicians, or venue management/staff. They tend to be all genders 18 and up, searching for performers globally or in the DC area, accessing the web on a variety of devices.
